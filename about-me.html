About Me

Pushpendra Singh Hada is an experienced & results-driven professional with a focus and specialization in the areas of Data Engineering, Analytics, MLops and Performance Improvement -data extraction, data quality check, transformation, ETL, cloud services, and Business Intelligence.

Pushpendra Singh Hada is currently pursuing Post Graduate Programme in Technology and Business Management from Masters' Union. This program brings together theoretical and practical knowledge to inculcate in students the skills required to survive and excel in the world of Business. This course is a unique blend of classroom engagement, educational tours, and boot camps meant to train us in every aspect of the Business world.

Pushpendra has worked as a Consultant in Tredence for FMCG clients based out of Europe. He has worked on modernizing the existing MLops workflow and optimized the entire process. This included understanding the existing process and providing recommendations and implementation to optimize and automate the MLops process. Apart from this, he has also worked on identified and piloted 2+ enhancements for in-house products, creating new revenue sources for the organization.

Pushpendra had worked as an Associate Consultant in Infosys for healthcare, e-commerce, and automobile clients based out of EMA. He has worked on the data migration project and ETL transformation projects and worked on Proof of Concept and the development of the optimal ETL Pipelines. Apart from this, he was part of the Infosys Flagship Program and was top 5% of all employees.
